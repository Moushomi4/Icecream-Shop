Delicious Ice Cream Recipes

Classic Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Whisk cream, milk, sugar, vanilla, and salt until sugar dissolves.
  2. Churn in an ice cream maker as per instructions.
  3. Freeze for 2 hours before serving.

Chocolate Fudge Swirl Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate fudge sauce
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Whisk all ingredients (except fudge) until smooth.
  2. Churn in ice cream maker.
  3. Add fudge sauce in last 5 mins for swirls.
  4. Freeze before serving.

Mango Sorbet

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe mangoes
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions:

  1. Puree mangoes until smooth.
  2. Heat sugar and water to make syrup. Cool it.
  3. Mix mango, syrup, lime juice.
  4. Churn and freeze.

Vegan Coconut Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans full-fat coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Whisk all ingredients until sugar dissolves.
  2. Churn in an ice cream maker.
  3. Freeze for 2+ hours before serving.
